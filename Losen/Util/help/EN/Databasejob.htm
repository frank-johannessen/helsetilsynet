Empty Recycle Bin

The Empty Recycle Bon option gives you the opportunity to schedule automatic emptying of the recycle bin. The aim of this function is to ensure that old information does not remain in the recycle bin for too long. The automatic emptying function empties the recycle bin of all information more than 30 days old. If you disable the automatic emptying function, you must empty the recycle bin manually by selecting Empty Recycle Bin in the toolbar in edit mode or by deleting individual pages in the recycle bin. When you empty the recycle bin manually, all information in the recycle bin is deleted.

Subscription

With the Subscription option, you can activate and set up how often the system is to send out e-mail messages to all visitors that have applied for subscription. Each visitor decides how often he/she wants to receive e-mail messages from the subscription function. You decide how often the system is to check for new and updated pages to distribute.

 

Archive function

Using the Archive function command, you activate and set how often the system should archive  information when the publication period has expired. Remember that there can be a delay between the time when the information stops being published and when it appears in the archive. This is because archiving is conducted perhaps once a day. In this case, it can take up to 23 hours and 59 minutes before the information appears in the archive.
