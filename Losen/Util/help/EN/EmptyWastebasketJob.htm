Automatic Emptying of Recycle Bin

The Automatic emptying of Recycle Bin option gives you the opportunity to schedule automatic emptying of the recycle bin. The aim of this function is to ensure that old information does not remain in the recycle bin for too long. The automatic emptying function empties the recycle bin of all information more than 30 days old. If you disable the automatic emptying function, you must empty the recycle bin manually by selecting Empty Recycle Bin in the toolbar in edit mode or by deleting individual pages in the recycle bin. When you empty the recycle bin manually, all information in the recycle bin is deleted.
