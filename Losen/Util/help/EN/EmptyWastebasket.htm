Empty Recycle Bin

When you have deleted pages from the structure, they are first placed in the recycle bin. The recycle bin should be emptied from time to time. It is possible to arrange automatic emptying of the recycle bin. Check with your system administrator what is applicable for your website. If this is not available, you have to empty the recycle bin yourself. To empty the recycle bin, click on the Empty Recycle Bin tool. Pages that are deleted from the recycle bin cannot be retrieved. A backup copy of the system's database has to be entered instead. Bear in mind that all editors share the same recycle bin, and if you empty it you delete all pages. If you want to delete one single page from the recycle bin, you do it in the same way as when you delete a page from the structure. 

Remove a page from the recycle bin

If you want to remove an individual page from the recycle bin without emptying all the contents it is done in the same way as when you remove a page from the structure. Mark the page in the recycle bin and select the tool Delete in the toolbar.

Restore a page from the recycle bin

The recycle bin is a separate tree structure under the Structure tab, and you can recognise it by the recycling symbol to the right. If you click on the plus symbol in front of the recycle bin you will see the content. To restore a page, move the page to the par t of the tree where you want the page to be. You can use any of the two different methods of moving a page.
